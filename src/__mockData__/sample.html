POOL PICKS WITH CONFIDENCE POINTS  

 

This is a pool like any other, except "confidence points" are added to show how confident a person is in a particular team to win. Example: During the regular season, there are normally 16 games in a week. Players submit a list of the teams they expect to win, and assign a number next to each team.

#16 = Highest Rating.....#1 = Lowest Rating

 

When a player assigns a #16 next to a team, that means he/she is most confident in that team to win....and so on down the line to #1, which means he/she is least confident in that pick.
